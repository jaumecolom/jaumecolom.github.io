 Bot2Bee
Automation of responses, interaction analysis, and real-time management.
Bot2Bee is an intelligent platform for creating chatbots on social media, designed to help businesses automate customer service and improve lead generation. Its functionality allows managing messages on Instagram, WhatsApp, and Messenger, with the option to intervene manually when needed. Ideal for physical or online businesses looking to promote themselves in a cost-effective and efficient way.

  • Frontend: Angular, HTML5, CSS3, TypeScript, Bootstrap.
  • Backend: Node.js, Express.js, Flask (Python)
  • Database: MongoDB
  • Infrastructure: AWS (Elastic Beanstalk, S3/CloudFront, EC2)
  • Integrations: Meta API (Messenger, WhatsApp, Instagram), Stripe, Make
